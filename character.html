Takao

Takao wares a white Admiral suit with short skirts, a white bow in her hair, a pair of black stockings, and a pair of lazy shoes of the same color. Her left hand holds a katana and at both of her sides she's equipped with an arsenal of artillery and torpedoes. Takao's ears do not stand up the same way her sister's do, instead they stick out at the sides.

Ark Royal

She wears a blue cropped jacket with red & yellow accents, a white & yellow collar and black garter straps on her arms over a white collared buttoned shirt with a cleavage cutout, a red slim & fit corset wrapped around her mid-section with yellow accents alongside a blue belt with yellow accents and circular golden buckle, black pencil skirt with a blue waist cape and a white wide garter, black thigh-high heeled boots with garter straps and white gloves.

Kawakaze

She has a waist-length silver hair with black-tipped wolf ears on top of her head, she has blue eyes and a silver wolf-like tail. She wears a white dress with a black sailor collar and a teal necktie, black high-waist skirt, black thigh-high boots and a detached black-trimmed miko-like sleeves with black ribbon scarfs and teal ribbon bows.
