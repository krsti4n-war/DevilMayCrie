Story Synopsis

In an alternate version of World War II, world is in chaos when Japan attacked US base in Pearl Harbour. Nuclear bomb was dropped after the attack as a response from US, causing Japan to surrender after the bombing.

Unbeknownst by both sides, the attack caused a radiation waste leakage to the sea. A mysterious enemy emerged from the sea because of the radiation effect. The enemy rapidly gained control of the sea, slowly crumbling the world down. The world decided to join forces in order to face against the mysterious being that has taken over the sea.
