REGISTER

Side scrolling action, revamped.

Each shipgirls have their own unique abilities, prompting players to build a custom fleet that consists of different ships from different countries.

ABOUT

Mysterious enemies lurk from the depth of sea. Navy from various countries shifted from bringing chaos to each other into uniting their power in order to bring peace back again.

CHARACTER

Hood, Royal Navy's Pride

Hood is a Battlecruiser from the Royal Navy and is part of the Admiral-class.
Hood is extremely elegant, proper, polite, and quite capable of defeating targets in a single volley.
Hood is a member of the Heroes Coalitions and is part of the Azur Lane Fleet.

SYSTEM

Auto Mode

Farm materials for your shipgirls without any trouble. Clear the stage with 3 stars and unlock the mode for the stage.
